🛡️ About Our Vision-Mask-X(Face Mask Detection & Alert System)

📌 Introduction

In today’s world, ensuring public health and safety has become more critical than ever. Our Real-Time Face Mask Detection & Alert System is designed to help monitor and enforce mask-wearing compliance in crowded areas, workplaces, and public spaces. Using computer vision and deep learning, this system can accurately detect individuals with or without masks and send alerts when violations occur.

💡 Why This Project?

With the ongoing challenges in public health safety, mask detection automation can:

  • Reduce the need for manual monitoring in crowded places.
  • Help businesses and organizations enforce safety guidelines effectively.
  • Provide data-driven insights into compliance trends over time.
  • Enhance public awareness by reinforcing mask-wearing habits.

📍 Applications

  • 🏢 Offices & Workspaces
  • 🏬 Shopping Malls & Supermarkets
  • 🏥 Hospitals & Healthcare Facilities
  • 🏫 Schools & Universities
  • 🚉 Public Transport Hubs
  • 🏭 Industrial & Manufacturing Units
